OPROMA OUR STORY


We, Oproma Cosmetics(Pvt)Ltd, is a GMP, ISO (9001-2015), and SLS 1672:2020 certified skincare, haircare and healthcare products manufacturing firm in Sri Lanka that distributes locally and exports internationally since 2012. We provide contract manufacturing services to a variety of well-known Sri Lankan firms. “The strength of the team is each individual member. The strength of each member is the team.” Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd was founded by Mr. Pradeep Mapalagama and Mrs. Ishani Gunaratne who have more than seventeen years of experience in the field of Beauty Care, Manufacturing, Marketing and Distributing. Our company Manufactures and Distributes Skincare, Healthcare, and Haircare Products. Our effort is to offer 100% result oriented, healthy and natural products for the benefit of our customers while making a valuable marketplace for Herbal and Natural ingredients in Sri Lanka. Since 2012, our company has maintained a well-reputed name along with the company brands; BLOOMING, OPLEE, and SHINEMO with a 100% satisfied customer base. The long-term business partners are proof of the quality supply and trustable service offered by our company. Our Contract Manufacturing process has led the way for the betterment of various companies while proving that our manufacturing plant supplies an excellent service throughout. Some of our main consumers are Baurs Life Sciences, JA Beauty Care, Ayendra and Co. (Pvt) Ltd and Chemsol (Pvt) Ltd. We are a corporation that goes to great lengths to ensure that Sri Lankan products are valued

ABOUT OPROMA


Our Vision

We strive to provide the highest level of transparency and customer satisfaction while causing no unnecessary harm to living or non-living beings. Our aim is to be Sri Lanka's No. 1 skincare, haircare, and healthcare products manufacturing company by ensuring the quality and safety of each and every product we manufacture.

Our Mission

We use Sri Lankan herbal heritage and modern cosmetology to achieve maximum commitment to product excellence. Our goal is to provide the best skincare, haircare, and healthcare products for the benefit of our customers, as well as to develop more products that are currently needed and to create product lines that are well-positioned within the current market while also attempting to minimise unemployment. We're a company that says what we're intending to do and then follows through. Transparency between ourselves and the consumer is a top priority for us.

Our Scope

The scope of the quality management system is narrowed to all the business functions carried out by Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd. The quality management system is primarily established for activities pertaining to the manufacturing of skincare, haircare, and healthcare products.

Quality policy

We, Oproma Cosmetics (Pvt) Ltd., are committed to providing quality skincare, haircare, and healthcare products to our customers by meeting their requirements and working in compliance with all applicable, regulatory, and statutory requirements. We are also committed to continually improving our systems, procedures, and resources.
